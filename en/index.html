About us

Mission

Our mission is to make photovoltaic energy even more sustainable and environmentally friendly than it already is. Through an innovative, highly efficient, and traceable technological process, we aim to close the loop of the photovoltaic energy production chain with the recovery and valorization of reusable materials from end-of-life photovoltaic panels.

Vision

We count on a renewable and sustainable energy future, based on the intelligent reuse of raw materials. Our project involves providing a transparent and traceable circular supply chain, where every decommissioned panel becomes an opportunity and a value for the energy and industrial sectors, as well as for the environment and the local territory.

Why choose RIUSA.TN

We offer a complete, reliable, traceable, and highly efficient recycling service. The use of advanced technology, tracked processes, and professional management of the entire workflow allow us to guarantee concrete and measurable results at every stage.
